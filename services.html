Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does the admissions process take?
+

It varies by programme, but most students work with us for 3–12 months. We recommend starting early to maximize your chances of placement and scholarship success.

Can I combine multiple services?
+

Absolutely. Most students use 2–3 services together. Our Pathfinder and Elite plans bundle services for a seamless experience at better value.

Do you work with students outside Nigeria?
+

Yes. While our core base is in Nigeria, we work with students across Africa and the diaspora. All sessions can be conducted virtually via Zoom or WhatsApp.

What is your SAT preparation success rate?
+

Students consistently achieve above-average scores. Many have improved by 200–400 points using our data-driven targeted study plans.

Is the initial consultation really free?
+

Yes — completely free with no obligation. We use this session to understand your goals, evaluate your profile, and recommend the most appropriate services.
