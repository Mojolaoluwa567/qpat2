Founder & Visionary

Temitope
Richard Fashakin

A social impact leader and entrepreneur committed to expanding access to education and opportunity. He is the founder of Giving Aid International Network (GAIN), an organization focused on empowering students as key agents of change through scholarships and educational support.

While an undergraduate at Obafemi Awolowo University, he facilitated international scholarship opportunities worth over $3 million, enabling students to study in the United States, United Kingdom, and Germany — many of whom have since become successful professionals abroad.

He later co-founded Qpat Solutions Limited with Dr. (Mrs.) Temilade Sesan, with a vision to provide affordable, high-quality educational and consulting services. Qpat Solutions delivers structured mentorship, data-driven academic preparation, and admissions consulting.

01

We don't just give advice — we walk alongside every student with intentional, structured support from first enquiry to final placement.

02
📊

Data-Driven Decisions

Progress is measured, not assumed. We apply performance analytics to track development and make informed decisions at every milestone.

03
🌍

Global Perspective

We prepare students for a world without borders — connecting Nigerian talent with opportunities in the US, UK, Canada, Germany and beyond.
